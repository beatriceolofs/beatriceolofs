Storön

About

This was a project that was going on during autumn 2020 for a course in Graphic design for web via Högskolan Kristianstad. The main task was to create a website & guide for tourists about an optional location. For this, I chose Storön located near Kalix, Sweden because it’s where I’m rooted. For this task I created a interactive prototype in Adobe XD with content about Storön. Illustrations are made in Adobe Illustrator. Photos used in the prototype are edited in Adobe Lightroom & Adobe Photoshop

About

During autumn 2019 and spring 2020 I was vice representative of the information committee for UMSYS, Umeås Medie & Systemvetarförening. The main responsibility was to publish information about UMSYS did for the students at Digital Mediaproduciton and the Systems Science Program studying at Umeå University. We published digital content on UMSYS site at facebook and website, created an Instagram and made a whole new graphic layout. I did the graphic layout, the website, and most custom Instagram & Facebook posts.

The website layout is made with HTML & CSS.
Other graphic content is made with Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop.
